Sanepa, Lalitpur

Kyampa, the social hub located in Sanepa, is known for serving absolutely divine food. With its fast service and great ambiance, it’s a perfect spot to unwind and enjoy a meal. The cheeseballs here are a standout—fluffy, cheesy, and melting in your mouth with every bite. The Mustang Aloo is another delight, packed with flavor. However, the highlight of the menu is the Dragon Chicken, which is perfectly crispy and absolutely delicious. The Timur Chicken and nachos are also worth trying, while the pizza, though decent, falls short of the high standards set by their other dishes.
