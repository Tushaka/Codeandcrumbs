Code & Crumbs

Code and Crumbs is a passionate group of five computer engineering students based in Nepal, united by their love for food and exploration. Founded in December 2024 during a spontaneous birthday treat. The group travels to various restaurants and cafes across Nepal to savor diverse cuisines. Their mission is to provide honest and detailed reviews, considering factors like taste, plating, service, price, and overall experience. Combining their technical expertise with a shared enthusiasm for culinary adventures, Code and Crumbs aims to guide food lovers toward the best dining spots while celebrating the vibrant food culture of Nepal.
