Newa Lahana serves the best Newari dishes. The buff choila was absolutely delicious, perfectly balanced with flavors. The anda bara was fluffy and delightful. The spice level of the aalu sadheko was spot on. Chatamari and yomari are timeless favorites that truly met our expectations. The chicken choila, while decent, could have been more flavorful.
